Meet Nadine Sheta

A virtual immigration attorney focused on delivering a polished, client-centered experience.

Nadine Sheta is the founder of NS Law Firm PLLC and a graduate of South Texas College of Law. Her practice focuses on immigration matters involving couples, families, and individuals seeking lawful status in the United States.

The firm is designed for clients who value professionalism, communication, and a clear process. With a fully virtual model, NS Law Firm PLLC combines convenience with a more refined, personalized legal experience.

Whether you are preparing a marriage-based case, a family petition, or a more complex immigration filing, the goal is simple: provide focused guidance in an organized, approachable environment.
